Available Variables:

Candidate
\{{candidate.firstname}} - Candidate's firstname eg. "Jon".
\{{candidate.fullname}} - Candidate's fullname eg. "Jon Smith".
Client
\{{client.name}} - Client's name eg. "Global {M}".
Job
\{{job.title}} - Job's tile eg. "Fullstack Developer".
\{{job.address.country}} - Office's Country eg. "England / UK".
\{{job.address.city}} - Office's city eg. "London".
\{{job.employmenttype}} - Job's contract type eg. "Permanent", "Contract" etc.
