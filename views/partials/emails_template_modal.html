Available Variables:

Candidate
\{{candidate.firstname}} - Candidate's firstname eg. "Jon".
\{{candidate.lastname}} - Candidate's lastname eg. "Smith."
\{{candidate.name}} - Candidate's fullname eg. "Smith".
Client
\{{client.name}} - Client's name eg. "Global {M}".
\{{client.country}} - Client's head office country eg. "England / UK".
\{{client.city}} - Client's head office city eg. "London".
\{{client.address}} - Client's head office full address eg. "89 Worship street, London, UK".
Job
\{{job.title}} - Job's tile eg. "Fullstack Developer".
\{{job.country}} - Office's Country eg. "England / UK".
\{{job.city}} - Office's city eg. "London".
\{{job.address}} - Office full address eg. "89 Worship street, London, UK".
\{{job.contract}} - Job's contract type eg. "Permanent", "Contract" etc.
